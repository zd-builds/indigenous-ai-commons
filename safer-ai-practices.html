Hierarchy of data risk

Not all AI tools carry the same risk to your data. The hierarchy of data risk framework -- specific to the threat model facing Indigenous Nations -- breaks down six tiers from US-based free models to local air-gapped machines, explains what legal compellability means at each tier, and includes five questions for evaluating any tool.
